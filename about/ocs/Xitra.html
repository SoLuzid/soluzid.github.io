Xitra

From: Parasites | Age: 19 | Height: 6'2" (188 cm) | she/they

About

Xitra is a quiet and reserved person who usually avoids attention. She can be shy around unfamiliar people, but becomes much more relaxed and friendly once she trusts someone.

Compared to her younger brother Atrix, she is much more careful and thinks things through before acting. She often ends up being the one who keeps him out of trouble.

Background

Xitra has a robotic arm and a robotic eye powered by the BlueSun, a powerful cyan-colored gem that generates massive amounts of energy.

Her arm contains a large star-shaped BlueSun core, allowing her to channel an incredible amount of power.

Trivia

  • She is transfem and bisexual.
  • She has red-green color blindness.
  • She is one year older than Atrix.
  • She is 6'2" (188 cm) tall.
  • She likes stars.
  • She dislikes showing her robotic arm and eye because she finds them embarrassing.
  • Her arm is pinkish because she thought the design looked cute, but she later started regretting the choice.
