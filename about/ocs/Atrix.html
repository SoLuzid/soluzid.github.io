Atrix Zeck

From: Parasites | Age: 18 | Height: 5'7" (170 cm) | he/him

About

Atrix is adventurous, but mostly because he has absolutely no sense of caution. He jumps into situations before thinking, usually causing problems that could have been avoided.

Despite being reckless and kind of a disaster, he's stubborn and refuses to give up. His older sister Xitra is usually the one keeping him alive and making sure he doesn't get himself killed.

Background

Atrix has a robotic arm and a robotic eye powered by the RedStar, a powerful ruby-colored gem capable of generating enormous amounts of energy.

He only uses a small portion of the RedStar's power because he understands how dangerous it can be if used carelessly.
